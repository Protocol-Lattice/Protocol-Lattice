About Protocol Lattice

An open organization for the layers around agents.

Protocol Lattice is an open-source organization building the layers around agent systems. Its repositories cover runtimes, memory, tool execution, service boundaries, and event-driven communication.

The projects stay focused and composable, so teams can choose a runtime, connect tools, and expose services without adopting one monolith.
